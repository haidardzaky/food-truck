About our food truck

Little Kittens

At the beginning of 2018, Little Kittens was born with our mobile cuisine, since then, Chef Dzaky, Angel and Fahri has been crafting high quality, Craft Burgers, Chicken Wings and the most recently, craft Milkshakes. Our food is handcrafted and made per order, which allows us to provide the highest quality food for you and your family.
